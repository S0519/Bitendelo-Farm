Organic plant-based food

Of all the organic foods available on the market, plant-based foods are the most well-known and recognised by consumers. Fruits, vegetables, cereals, grains, nuts, mushroom and seaweed, among others, can all fall under the category of organic foods, but they can only be called organic when produced according to rules laid down in the organic regulatory framework. Organic farming offers an alternative agricultural system. It initially began in the early in the 20th century. The advent of pesticides brought in its benefits and pitfalls simultaneously. It is more healthy relying on compost manure, green manure, and bone meal.
